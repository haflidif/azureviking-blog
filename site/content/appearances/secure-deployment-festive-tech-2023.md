## About This Talk

In this festive-themed session, I walk through the essentials of secure deployments in Azure, combining holiday spirit with serious security practices.

## Topics Covered

- **Infrastructure as Code Security** — Scanning and validating your Terraform/Bicep templates before deployment
- **Pipeline Hardening** — Securing your CI/CD pipelines against common attack vectors
- **Deployment Best Practices** — Blue/green deployments, canary releases, and rollback strategies
- **Identity & Access** — Using managed identities and least-privilege principles in your deployment pipelines
